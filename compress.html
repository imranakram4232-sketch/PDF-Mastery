About PDF Compression

Our PDF compression tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce your PDF file size while maintaining the best possible quality. You can choose between different compression levels depending on your needs:

  • Low Compression: Minimal size reduction, best for documents where quality is critical
  • Medium Compression: Balanced approach, recommended for most documents
  • High Compression: Significant size reduction, some quality loss may be noticeable
  • Extreme Compression: Maximum size reduction, best for web use or email attachments

All processing happens in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.
